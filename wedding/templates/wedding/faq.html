RSVP, Kids & Plus Ones

When should I RSVP by?

Please RSVP by July 24, 2026 so we can finalize seating and catering counts.

How do I RSVP?

Use the RSVP page. Look up your invite by email or by name, then complete the form for everyone in your party.

Can I bring a plus-one?

Plus-ones are listed by name on your invitation. Your RSVP will show how many guests your invitation covers — please RSVP for that number or fewer.

Are kids welcome?

We love your kids — but we've chosen to keep our wedding an adults-only celebration unless your children are listed by name on your invitation.

What if my plans change after I RSVP?

Just send us a note as soon as you know. The earlier the better, especially after the RSVP deadline.

Event Details

When and where is the wedding?

Saturday, September 5, 2026, at The Skyline, 707 Dawson St, San Antonio, TX 78202. Ceremony begins at 5:30 PM.

What time should I arrive?

Please plan to be seated at least 15 minutes before the ceremony start time.

What's the dress code?

Cocktail attire. Please leave the jeans and sneakers at home — but comfortable shoes for the dance floor are very welcome.

Is the ceremony indoors or outdoors?

Both portions of the day will primarily be indoors with some open-air areas for cocktails. We'll keep an eye on the September Texas weather and send updates as needed.

How long will the reception last?

Reception runs until 11:00 PM. We hope you'll dance with us all night.

Travel & Parking

What airport should I fly into?

San Antonio International (SAT) is closest — about 15 minutes from the venue.

Is there a hotel block?

Yes — see the Accommodations page for booking details and deadlines.

Where do I park?

On-site and street parking are both available near the venue. Use the map below to plan your route from a nearby restaurant we recommend.

Do you recommend Uber/Lyft or driving?

Either works. If you plan to enjoy a few drinks (please do!), rideshare is the easy choice.
