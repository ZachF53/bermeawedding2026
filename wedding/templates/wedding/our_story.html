Brian & Aisha Wedding

05 SEP 2026

Like many modern love stories, ours began with a swipe—on Hinge, of all places. What started as hopeful curiosity quickly turned into something unforgettable.

By our second date, we found ourselves at a coffee shop, completely lost in conversation—and in a 1,000-piece puzzle. Hours slipped by as we talked about life, family, dreams, and everything in between. We didn't notice the time until the staff gently let us know they were closing for the day. By then, we knew we'd found something rare. Something real.

Aisha is a fierce and brilliant attorney with a heart as strong as it is generous. Brian is a compassionate nurse who enjoys navigating chaos and bringing laughter to every room he enters. What made our story truly ours was the moment love expanded beyond the two of us.

Not only did Brian fall in love with Aisha—he stepped into the beautiful, brave role of becoming a father figure to Kai. Watching their bond grow has been one of the greatest joys of our lives.

Our little family has continued to grow—first with a curious cat, Mango, then a spirited puppy, Apollo. There's always a bit of fur, a lot of laughter, and even more love in our home.

Now, in 2026, we're ready to say "I do"—to a life full of puzzle pieces, coffee shop moments, and the kind of love that only deepens with time. We can't wait to celebrate this next chapter with all of you.
